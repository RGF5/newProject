How do we teach?

Technology has allowed us to cross the world and connect with new people. Our teaching will be done online, and that has many benefits to it; • Flexibility with your schedule, and the virtual platform used • Bringing education to your home or wherever you wish to learn, maybe on vacation. • Meet new people from around the world • Multiple virtual platforms (Skype/Whatsapp/Facebook etc..) < Once we assess your abilities from the free consultation and give you a summary of how we can assist you in growing with the language. You will be provided with a package tailored specifically for you, and the areas you will work on. The schedule will be built around yours, and we will guide you throw a wonderful learning experience online.
